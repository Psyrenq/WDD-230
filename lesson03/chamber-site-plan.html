Site Purpose
The purpose of this website is to be used as an introduction to Fort Wayne, a small city located in the midwestern part of United States. For families and individuals who have recently moved to the city, they can information on the city's history, information on current events, and new things happening around the city.
Wireframe

Typography
For this site, I've chosen 'Roboto, Sans-sarif'. I believe it presents a bold, readable text that works well against the color scheme of the site. It's friendly and easy on the eyes.
